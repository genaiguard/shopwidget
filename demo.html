How to Integrate ShopSpec

To add this chat widget to your real webshop, simply add this script tag to your HTML:

<script
    src="https://your-github-username.github.io/shopspec-widget/shopspec-widget.js"
    data-api-key="your-perplexity-api-key-here">
</script>

The widget will automatically:

  • Detect your domain (e.g., yourshop.com)
  • Only recommend products from your site
  • Maintain conversation context
  • Provide clickable links to your product pages
